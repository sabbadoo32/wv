Project Summary

We looked at four weather factors (temperature, humidity, windspeed and visibility) at 1000 cities around the globe. Using a combination of Pandas, Python, HTML, Matplotlib and CSS; we created visualizations to help you understand the intracicies and symmetry of global weather.


This project is part of my Northwestern University Data Science Bootcamp curriculum and is hosted at Github.io; part of a second project.
